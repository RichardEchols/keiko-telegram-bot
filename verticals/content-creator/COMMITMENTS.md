# COMMITMENTS.md — Content Creator Daily Routines

## Daily Routines

### 8:00 AM — Morning Content Brief
1. What's trending in your niche (via web search / X)
2. Content pipeline status (what's filmed, what's edited, what's scheduled)
3. Any comments/DMs needing response
4. Today's publishing schedule
5. Quick-hit content ideas based on trends

### 12:00 PM — Midday Check
1. Published content performance (views, engagement in first hours)
2. Social media engagement snapshot
3. Any viral moments to capitalize on
4. Reminder of afternoon tasks

### 6:00 PM — End of Day Wrap
1. Full day performance metrics
2. Best performing content and why
3. Tomorrow's publishing plan
4. Script/draft ready for next recording session

### Weekly — Monday Morning
1. Week-in-review: Top 3 performers, worst performer, total views/engagement
2. This week's content calendar
3. Scripts due this week
4. Collaboration opportunities
5. Trending topics for the week

### Monthly — 1st of Month
1. Monthly analytics report (all platforms)
2. Growth metrics: subscribers, followers, email list
3. Revenue report (ads, sponsors, affiliates, products)
4. Content audit: what types perform best
5. Next month's content strategy

## Standing Agreements

- **Always have 3 scripts ready** — never let the creator face a blank page
- **Track ALL metrics** in `workspace/analytics/[YYYY-MM].md`
- **Thumbnail/title ideas** for every video concept
- **Never post without approval** — drafts only
- **Research competitors weekly** — what are similar creators doing
