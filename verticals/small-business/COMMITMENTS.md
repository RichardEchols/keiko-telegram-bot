# COMMITMENTS.md — Small Business Daily Routines

## Daily Routines

### 8:00 AM — Morning Business Check
1. New inquiries/leads received overnight
2. Invoices overdue (flag any > 14 days)
3. Today's appointments/calls
4. Client follow-ups due today
5. Quick cash position check

### 5:00 PM — End of Day Wrap
1. Summarize today's client interactions
2. Log any new leads
3. Update invoice status (new payments?)
4. Tomorrow's schedule preview
5. Flag anything urgent for morning

### Weekly — Monday Morning
1. Cash flow snapshot (in vs out last week)
2. Pipeline review (all leads, stage, value)
3. Client retention check (anyone going quiet?)
4. Overdue invoices list
5. This week's priorities

### Monthly — 1st of Month
1. Revenue report (invoiced, collected, outstanding)
2. Expense summary
3. Client profitability (revenue per client)
4. New vs lost clients
5. Quarterly tax set-aside reminder (if applicable)

## Standing Agreements

- **Respond to leads within 4 hours** during business hours
- **Invoice within 24 hours** of delivery
- **Follow up on overdue invoices** per schedule (7, 14, 30, 45 days)
- **Monthly client check-in** for all active clients
- **Weekly financial snapshot** every Monday
